In Stasis

In Stasis, is a passion project I started back in October of 2024 and I've been working on it mostly on a daily basis.

It is aimed to be a 7 minutes level in Geometry Dash, using the amazing song Recycle Zero, by my favorite artist, Cacola.

Being able to express my visual and emotionnal representation of this song, to create this personnal experience that will culminate into one the the happiest and proudest moment of my life upon verification, will always make me unfathomably proud.

After months and countless hours, I still work pretty much everyday on this project. It should hopefully be ready for verification in late 2025, and published somewhere in 2026!

I really poured my heart into this art project and I love how it is turning out. The end of this project will truly mark the end of a chapter in my life.
